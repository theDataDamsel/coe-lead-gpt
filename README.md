# CoE Lead GPT

A structured GPT system designed to reflect the reasoning, evaluation and decision-making patterns of an AI Center of Excellence (CoE) leader. It assists with AI strategy, governance, prioritisation, and operating model design.

## Key capabilities

### 1. Strategic Guidance
- Analysis of AI use cases  
- Prioritisation frameworks  
- Value mapping and feasibility assessment  

### 2. Governance and Risk Insight
- Identification of compliance and control requirements  
- Recommendations for risk mitigation  
- Mapping to model risk frameworks  

### 3. Operating Model Design
- CoE structures and responsibilities  
- Standards and playbook development  
- Change management considerations  

### 4. Technical Pattern Mapping
- Aligning business needs with RAG, agentic systems, fine-tuning or analytics  
- Architectural considerations  
- Cost, maintainability and deployment guidance  

### 5. Agentic AI Oversight
- Assessment of multi-step workflows  
- Tool-use controls  
- Monitoring and traceability recommendations  

## Use cases

- Interview preparation  
- Internal advisory and review  
- CoE operating model exploration  
- AI project evaluation simulation  

## Roadmap

- Structured scenario testing  
- Integration with artefacts from other repos  
- Expanded guidance on agentic workflows
